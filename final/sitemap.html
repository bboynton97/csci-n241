    Career          Platform        Donate           Blog

Career

The career page will be separated into 3 columns with `ol` elements nested inside the columns. Each column will represent a period of the General's life and list his achievements. This page is intended to demonstrate the General's successful track record and instill a sense of trust in his supporters.


Platform

The platform page will consist of a table with ratings on all of the Galaxy's hot-button issues. It will rank his position on each topic as well as a reason for his position. This page should be very straightforward and make sure any user fully understands the General's political platform.


Donate

The donate page consists of a payment form. This will ask for the user's name, address, phone number, payment info and how much they are willing to pay.


Blog

The blog page will have a handful of articles related to the progress of the campaign. These articles will be written to boost morale and support for the General.
