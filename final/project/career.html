Rick's Career


Military

  1. Graduated top of his class a the Smlemfark Spaceforce Academy on Earth D-184.
  2. Entered the Citadell of Ricks Spaceforce at the age of 22 and quickly rose through the ranks.
  3. By the age of 32 Rick was the sitting commander at the Citadell of Ricks.

Business

  1. While on R&R from the Spaceforce at the age of 27, Rick started his business Federation. Rick began a small company known today as "Plumbus Co".
  2. The plumbus, while origionally designed for the military, was quickly becoming popular amongst the civilian population.
  3. After the announcement of the Plumbus X, sales skyrocketed and Commander Rick made his first billion Blemflarks.
  4. Under the loose guidance of Rick and his daughter, Commander Rick Industries, the parent of Plumbus Co, is set to be the first Trillion Blemflark business.

Political

  1. After Commander Rick's successes in both the military and business, his election to the Council of Ricks was not a surprise. After announcing his candidacy, Rick aquired over 80% of the Ricks' votes.
  2. While holding his seat on the Council of Ricks, Commander Rick maintained an approval rating of 99%.
  3. As a man of the people, recent calls for Rick's ascension in polical power have convinced Rick to run for the highest office in the Galactic Federation.
